BONUS · Regular Verbs Rule
Which of these is the correct Past Simple of "study"?
✅ Verbs ending in consonant + y → change y to i and add -ed. study → studied, cry → cried, try → tried
BONUS · Regular Verbs Rule
Which is the correct Past Simple of "stop"?
✅ Short verb ending in vowel + consonant → double the last letter: stop → stopped, run → ran (irregular)
🔧 Past Simple Grammar

עבר פשוט – שאלות ושלילה. פתור את התרגילים!

Q1 · Positive Sentence
Write the correct form: She ______ (go) to the marketplace yesterday.
כתוב את הצורה הנכונה: היא ______ לשוק אתמול.
Q2 · Positive Sentence
Write the correct form: They ______ (bring) fresh vegetables to the center.
Q3 · Negative Sentence
Make it negative: She visited the playground. → She ______ the playground.
הפוך לשלילי: היא ביקרה במגרש המשחקים. → היא ______ במגרש.
Q4 · Yes/No Question
Form a Yes/No question: They cooked meat. → ______ they cook meat?
צור שאלת כן/לא: הם בישלו בשר. → ______ הם בישלו בשר?
Q5 · WH Question
She found a special carpet. → What ______ she find?
היא מצאה שטיח מיוחד. → מה היא מצאה?
Q6 · was / were
The reporter ______ (be) very special in our neighborhood.
Q7 · Multiple Choice – Spot the Error
Which sentence is CORRECT?
✅ After didn't → use BASE FORM of the verb. Not "went" or "goes" — just "go"!
Q8 · Multiple Choice – Choose the correct sentence
Which is the correct Yes/No question?
✅ Did + subject + BASE FORM. "enjoy" not "enjoyed" — DID already shows past tense!
📰 Reading Comprehension

קרא את הקטע וענה על השאלות.

The Green Neighborhood Project

Last spring, a group of students decided to clean up their neighborhood. They collected trash from the streets and planted trees in the playground. A reporter from the local newspaper came to write about the project. She said it was the most special story of the year.

The students also visited an old woman named Mrs. Katz. She lived in the center of the neighborhood and enjoyed cooking. She baked bread and cooked a stew with fresh vegetables and meat. The students helped her fill the shopping bags at the marketplace.

At the end of the day, they were tired but happy. They knew they saved electricity by walking instead of driving. Mrs. Katz said, "You are the future of this neighborhood!"
Q1 · True or False
The students planted flowers in the playground. (True / False?)
✅ FALSE! They planted trees, not flowers. Check the passage!
Q2 · Multiple Choice
Who came to write about the project?
✅ "A reporter from the local newspaper came to write about the project."
Q3 · Open Answer
Where does Mrs. Katz live? Write a full sentence.
Q4 · Why? (Cause & Effect)
Why did the students save electricity?
✅ "They saved electricity by walking instead of driving." – Cause = walking. Effect = saved electricity!
Q5 · Vocabulary in Context
Find a word in the passage that means "unique / not ordinary".
Q6 · Did question
Did Mrs. Katz enjoy cooking? Answer in a full sentence.
האם גב' כץ נהנתה לבשל? ענה במשפט מלא.

🔗 Cause & Effect Matching

חבר סיבה לתוצאה. בחר את ההמשך הנכון לכל משפט.

Q1
She took a shower, so...
✅ Taking a quick shower = saving water! "Take a shower" is one of your vocabulary words.
Q2
He didn't clean up the neighborhood, so...
✅ If you DON'T clean up → result is dirty streets. Cause + Effect logic!
Q3
They planted trees in the park, because...
✅ "Protect" and "plant" go together with environmental care. The verb "protects" is in your vocabulary list!
